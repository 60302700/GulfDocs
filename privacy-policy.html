Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

Overview

GulfDocs is a free, no-signup document generator. This policy explains what happens to your information when you use it. The short version: your document data (company details, client details, line items, custom templates) is processed entirely in your browser and is never sent to or stored on our servers.

Information we do not collect

There is no account creation, login, or payment on GulfDocs, so we never collect:

  • Your name, email, or any registration details
  • Payment or billing information
  • The content of the invoices, payslips, quotations, purchase orders, billing details or custom templates you create

The web server that serves this site only sends you the static page files (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) and the demo document routes used for preview purposes. It does not receive or store the data you type into any form.

Local storage on your device

To make it convenient to come back to an in-progress document, GulfDocs saves your form entries and any custom template you upload to your browser's local storage — a storage area built into your browser that lives only on your device. This data:

  • Is never transmitted to us or any third party
  • Stays on your device until you clear it (via your browser settings, or by clearing the corresponding fields in the app)
  • Is not covered by a "right to deletion" request to us, because we never had a copy of it — clearing your own browser storage is the way to remove it

Cookies & advertising

GulfDocs is, or will be, funded by advertising rather than charging users. At the time of writing, no advertising scripts are active on the site. When we do enable an ad network (such as Google AdSense), that network may set cookies or use similar technology in your browser to:

  • Show ads relevant to you
  • Measure how ads perform
  • Limit how many times you see the same ad
The cookie banner shown on this site lets you choose "Necessary only" or "Accept all" before any advertising cookies are set. Once ads are live, this policy will be updated with the specific ad network(s) in use and links to their own privacy policies and opt-out tools (for example, Google's Ads Settings and the Google Partner Sites policy).

Third-party fonts

This site loads fonts from Google Fonts' CDN. Loading a font from Google's servers means your browser makes a direct request to Google, which can see your IP address and browser details as part of that request, independent of anything GulfDocs does. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Children's privacy

GulfDocs is a business-document tool intended for general business/professional use and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your choices

  • Clear your browser's local storage / site data at any time to remove anything GulfDocs has saved on your device.
  • Use the cookie banner (or your browser's cookie settings) to control advertising cookies once ads are enabled.
  • Use your browser's "Do Not Track" or ad-blocking settings — GulfDocs's core document-generation features work the same either way.

Changes to this policy

If how GulfDocs handles data changes — most likely when advertising is switched on — this page will be updated and the "Last updated" date above will change accordingly.
